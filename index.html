Help Center

How do I get started with LightLogic?

To begin using LightLogic, simply download our app, create an account, and follow the setup instructions to connect your devices.

Can I control LightLogic from multiple devices?

Yes, you can control your smart lighting system from multiple smartphones or tablets by logging in with the same account credentials.

Do I need a hub to use LightLogic products?

No, LightLogic devices communicate directly with your Wi-Fi network, eliminating the need for a separate hub.

What smart home platforms are compatible with LightLogic?

LightLogic integrates seamlessly with popular smart home platforms including Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple HomeKit.

How can I troubleshoot connectivity issues?

If you're experiencing connectivity issues, try rebooting your router, ensuring that your devices are within range, and updating the LightLogic app to the latest version.

Can I schedule lighting scenes with LightLogic?

Yes, you can create customized lighting schedules to automate your home's lighting based on your preferences and routines.
